Web Design, Search Engine Optimization, Mobile Device Readiness

DeW-itt-Tech is a family-owned North Carolina Internet Marketing company concentrating on small businesses located in western North Carolina and east Tennessee. We have been in business since 2003.

In 2001, it was completely sufficient for us to place a business listing on the first page of a Google search.  Phones rang.  Not today.  People do not exclusively use Google anymore.  Today, more people use mobile devices to find what they want to do and what they want to know.  Web sites must be responsive to smaller screens.  Businesses must be listed in the many and varying mobile business directories.  The folks at Yelp and at Google are still enjoying their market shares.  Getting your business to appear where people ae going on the Internet is still a moving target.

The folks at DeW-itt-Tech are proud that we have been continually measuring the effectiveness of what's going on with the Internet.  We use systems that can control the outcomes in areas deemed uncontrollable in the past.  We can bury a bad review.  We can get a business on nearly every mobile directory in the American market.  

Our handle on the search engines has long been proven to be superior.

There has been a stong tendancy for business owners to go to the free platforms such as Wix, Weebly, and Go Daddy's Site Builder in order to get away from paying exorbitant fees to guys (like us) in the web site building business.  We applaud that movement.  Most business owners who have the time and the patience to pull this off seem to get stuck on landing on search engines.  Let's face it - if you are not on search engines, you are not on the Internet.  DeW-itt-Tech gurantees our work with Search Engine optimization.  Most business owners lack the ability to make videos.

This is not an easy world.  The ingredients needed to be visible to so many millios of people looking for answers on the Internet is and endless list of moving targets.  We mix our experience with Google, Yahoo, Bing, and MSN with our experience using the very best mobile device systems.
